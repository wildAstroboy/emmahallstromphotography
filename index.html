Who Am I

(and why we're already best friends)

One thing youll learn about me during our time together is that I LOVE love.

I love all types. Newlywed love, sibling love, the love between a grumpy grandpa and all his grandkids, and the love between parents (especially seen when wrangling kids for one nice picture). No matter what season of life you're in, you have a story to tell, and love to show. I'm here to capture that.

So now that ive shown you what I love, show me yours. Let's capture your love story together. I have a feeling it's going to be a good one.
